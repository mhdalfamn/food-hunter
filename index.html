Know Us

Significant to its name, Food Hunter is located on the 38th floor boasting a 360-degree city view to complement the elegance of its dining areas, bar and lounge, along with lively show kitchens which serve international cuisine featuring Western and traditional Malay style. The captivating panoramic view, the dramatic sense of décor and the splendour of its cuisine are what makes Food Hunter the signature restaurant of the hotel. Whether to entertain business associates or celebrate special occasions with family and friends, Food Hunter is the place to be.

The safety and wellbeing of our guests and colleagues is always a top priority for us.

In light of COVID-19-related government mandates, Food Hunter services and operating hours may be modified until further notice.

In view of overwhelming patronage we have received for afternoon tea and dinner, Food Hunter may encounter full-house situations where seats will not be available for walk-ins. To avoid disappointments and to help us manage all expectations, we strongly recommend advanced reservations to be made for dine-ins at Food Hunter.
